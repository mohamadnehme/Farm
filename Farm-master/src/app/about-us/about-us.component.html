About Us

"Digital Farmer" is a platform engaging a crowdfunding approach that goes-between investors and entrepreneurs, catalyzing the fundraising of agriculture projects in Lebanon. In other words, it plays the role of the financial mediator by gathering donations for poor and needy businesses, creating new momentum for economic development, and accelerating the transition towards smart farming to build a financially and food secured future.

Our Objectives

  • Crowdfunding farms with no Payback, thus liberation from heavy loans and financial stress.
  • Submitting simple alternative financing approach to traditional funding.
  • Boosting and lifting small farming businessesâ€™ productivity.
  • Managing and organizing the agricultural investment network.
  • Planting a competitive sense in the Lebanese agri-business sector.
