How It Works

We handle the entire agric value chain "from start to finish" while you sit back and enjoy returns after harvest

AN INDUSTRY THAT MATTERS

Ownership in a farm operation allows investors to learn about the many aspects that go into a real working farm and the hard work that goes into feeding the world.

DEMAND FOR FOOD

By 2050 the world population will grow to over 10 billion people. An additional 3 billion people will enter the middle class causing food demand to sky rocket.

Fundraising Scarcity in the Lebanese Agri-Sector

The Lebanese agricultural sector become sleepier than before with the running economic and financial crisis. As no formal agricultural credit is available, fundraising farming projects becomes a must.

MORE STABILITY THAN THE STOCK MARKET

Since farmland is uncorrelated with most other major asset classes, it has proven to perform well during challenging economic conditions.

Building Healthy Relationships

Once a quarter, we hold a #VillageMeeting enabling our digital farmers see how their sponsorship is affecting real people and effecting economic growth.

Secured Sponsorship

Your sponsorship with us is secured. We have a relationship with Leadway Assurance Plc.

OUR MISSION

The mission of “Digital Farmer” company is to smooth the funding process of poverty-stricken farming programs through adopting a simple alternative financing approach that builds an interrelated investment network towards energizing the dozy Lebanese agricultural sector.
